National Parkinson Foundation and Global Kinetics Corporation Announce Commencement of Clinical Trial
 
 
 
Global Kinetics Corporation and the US based National Parkinson Foundation (NPF) will collaborate on a clinical research project to study the impact of continuous objective measurement of movement in patients with Parkinson’s disease using the Personal KinetiGraph™ (PKG™) Movement Recording System.
 
This clinical research project will be a multi-center randomized controlled trial involving over 400 Parkinson’s patients conducted as part of the NPF Parkinson’s Outcomes Project Registry Study.
 
By including the PKG™ System as an element of routine care, the study will consider the use of continuous, precise and accurate assessments of patient’s movement based symptoms among the other factors that ultimately produce variability in the use of medicines and treatment plans, and likely paths toward better outcomes for people with Parkinson’s.
 
The National Parkinson’s Foundation is the second national patient advocacy organisation with whom Global Kinetics Corporation is formally collaborating and we anticipate making additional announcements on new projects in the near future.
