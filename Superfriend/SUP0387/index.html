Let's Talk About Suicide
 
 
You are invited to participate in SuperSPAN, SuperFriend’s Suicide Prevention Program. The program is suitable for anyone working in a role that has interactions with people who are at risk of, or affected by suicide.
The Program includes a two-day workshop followed by monthly teleconferences (1hour in duration) for up to three months (date/time TBA). It provides participants with the skills and confidence to recognise warning signs of mental illness and provide potentially life-saving support to those in need.
The program will be presented by Ms Susan Beaton. Ms Beaton is a Psychologist, Suicide Prevention Specialist and Mindfulness Coach, who has been working in the field of Suicide Prevention field for 30 years across Australia and the USA. Ms Beaton has been working in non-government organisations in training and education development and delivery, policy development, research and evaluation and strategic planning.
Ms Beaton will be supported by:
 
• Ms Fiona Livingstone, Project Co-ordinator, Centre for Rural and Remote Mental Health at the University of Newcastle
• Dr Alison Kennedy, Research Fellow for the Ripple Effect, National Centre for Farmer Health
• Louise Flynn, Manager at Support After Suicide – Jesuit Social Services
