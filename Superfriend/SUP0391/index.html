Dr Laura Kirby Director & Principal Psychologist - CommuniCorp Group

Dr Laura Kirby is a Director and Principal Psychologist of CommuniCorp Group, and has extensive experience in workplace training, organisational development and workplace wellbeing initiatives, across a range of organisations and sectors.

Laura specialises in a wide range of workplace and professional capabilities including resilience, wellbeing, workplace psychological health and interpersonal capability. With a background in organisational psychology and mental health, Laura has developed her expertise in researching, assessing and understanding stress and psychological health and engaging appropriate intervention and prevention strategies for individuals, teams and the broader organisation.

In her role at CommuniCorp Laura also manages large-scale and national programs for a broad range of private and public sector organisations. Laura focusses on the development and delivery of evidence-based, practical and engaging training and organisational development solutions.
