Research tells us that, due to its prevalence, mental illness is affecting your business right now, or has the capacity to do so. At any point in time, one in six working aged Australians are living with a mental illness.

Beyond that, it is believed there are more people experiencing mental health challenges ranging from high stress to undiagnosed depression. If your organisation isn't incorporating mental health considerations into your business strategy, then you are falling behind, in terms of both your workplace culture and the costs you are adding to your business.

This webinar will look at what being mentally healthy really means for your business.
 

Elisa Walther

Elisa is a highly experienced psychologist from Communicorp Group and has more than 20 years' experience consulting to and working within large organisations, focusing on effective people practices. Elisa's expertise lies in the development and delivery of strategic people solutions, effectual training programs, coaching and workplace interventions to help support the psychological health and sustainable productivity of individuals and teams within a business, as well as the broader organisation.

She has extensive experience both consulting to national and global organisations across diverse industries, as well as working within organisations as part of the People & Culture, organisational development and leadership teams, giving her a strong appreciation for the commercial realities and internal pressures within an organisation. Elisa is an engaging coach and presenter who utilises her psychological expertise to translate best practice and evidence based research and information into a practical format.
