Amgen is proud to announce the launch of an exciting new education resource for cancer nurses in Australia. With input from the CNSA and with the assistance of an independent steering committee, the series of accredited online modules will explore the use of innovative therapies in many different forms of cancer and how nurses can prepare and assist cancer patients in the future.
 
The first module "Innovative therapies in cancer care: Moving beyond the traditional chemotherapy era" is endorsed by ACN according to their Continuing Professional Development (CPD) Endorsed Course Standards. It has been allocated 1 CPD hour according to the Nursing and Midwifery Board of Australia-Continuing Professional Development Standard.
 
Cancer nurses can visit AmgenProfessional.com.au to register and complete this module.
