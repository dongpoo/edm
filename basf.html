We are pleased to be launching an online training program for Systiva seed treatment. It is a simple way to get to know more about the benefits of Systiva and the role it can play in optimizing barley production.

 

As you are no doubt aware, Systiva delivers powerful disease control against seed, soil and foliar diseases in barley crops. It's been shown to enhance plant health with vigorous growth in roots and shoots, providing a robust foundation for crop establishment.

The training program is part of BASF's best practice stewardship of Systiva. It explains how Systiva works, its benefits on crop yields, and how it can be used as part of a resistance management program. There is a short, simple quiz at the end to test how well you've taken everything on board!
