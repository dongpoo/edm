Update to your Roundup® Ready canola License and Stewardship Agreement (LSA)
What has changed?
A review of the LSA has resulted in a few changes to the terms and conditions of the agreement and we have summarised these below for your information:
• A two-day notice period, wherever the agreement grants Monsanto (or its representative) access to your crops for regulatory auditing and compliance purposes.
• As per LSA clauses 1.18 and 1.19, the Office of the Gene Technology Regulator (OGTR), does not need to provide notice to access crops under license DIR120, covering the cultivation of Roundup Ready canola and Triazine Tolerant Roundup Ready canola (TT-RR canola) Crops.
