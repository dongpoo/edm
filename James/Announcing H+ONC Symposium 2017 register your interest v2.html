Translating the paradigm of drug discovery and development to everyday clinical practice
 
Amgen Australia is proud to present the H+ONC Symposium 2017 – Bringing Discovery Science to Life – an educational event featuring eminent national and international speakers who will deliver a cutting-edge, clinically-relevant educational program focusing on human therapeutics in haematology and oncology. We want to share with you current opinion and future trends from discovery science, through translational research, to therapeutic outcomes.
 
The symposium is being held at the Grand Hyatt, Melbourne, on Friday 5th May with a symposium dinner followed by a full day of sessions on Saturday 6th May 2017. The educational program will be made up of joint plenary sessions and parallel haematology and oncology streams. In addition, we are delighted to annouce that there will be a session specifically for advanced trainees held on Friday afternoon. Details to come...
 
When: Friday evening 5th May & Saturday 6th May 2017
Advanced Trainees Friday afternoon 5th May
 
Where: The Grand Hyatt, 123 Collins St, Melbourne
 
The agenda and speakers will be announced in early 2017. Register your interest to receive early notification.
